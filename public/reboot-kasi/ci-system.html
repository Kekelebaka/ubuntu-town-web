01 — The Strategy

We don't change everything. We correct the skin and keep the substance.

You asked: do we change all, keep what matters, or update all? The honest answer — the strategy and structure are right; the colours I first improvised were not your brand. So we keep the thinking, fix the identity, and re-skin every surface onto the real CI.

Keep — it's working

The substance

  • The OS model — Signal → Workpack → Proof → Memory
  • All content & IA across both sites
  • Kopano governed-AI + the coordinator model
  • The ecosystem — nothing removed
  • The family motif + each sub-brand's own logo
Change — I got it wrong

The identity

  • Improvised clay-red primary → real Ubuntu purple #6B1F66
  • Flat white → warm cream canvas
  • Add proper light + dark (default light)
  • Align every accent to the real sub-brand hexes
  • Surface “an initiative of Abantu Bobuntu”
Update — apply everywhere

All surfaces

  • Re-skin the public front door
  • Re-skin the coordinator portal
  • Re-skin the command plan
  • Add the light/dark toggle to each
  • Swap ecosystem chips to real brand colours

03 — The Mark

The family, in an arch

The primary logo is the Ubuntu Town crest — a township beneath a church spire, a family on roads that fan out like opportunity, banner-locked in purple and orange. Its heart is the family motif: figures big and small, together — I am because we are. For tight spaces, a simplified family-mark serves as the app icon.

You in. We rise.
✓ Do
  • Purple + orange lockup
  • Warm cream or violet-black grounds
  • Arch + circle-head motif
  • Script only for taglines
✗ Don't
  • Clay-red or flat primaries
  • Pure #FFF / #000 grounds
  • Stretch or recolour sub-brand logos
  • Script for body copy
Relationship
