01 — The model

Self-funding by design: income is a share, not a salary

There is no donor payroll to run out. Coordinators convert spazas into Opportunity Points and stand up nodes; those earn real revenue; a fixed share of that revenue pays the people who built it, and another slice is reinvested to fund the next wave. The flywheel funds itself.

The ramp bridge

Month 1 has little revenue, so an activation stipend (paid per verified Opportunity Point, per node stood up, and per approved workpack) bridges months 1–3 from a small launch fund. It is activity-based and self-limiting — as your town's revenue-share grows, the stipend phases out. You are never on a fixed wage; you're always earning from what you build.

04 — How pay is set

Remuneration = role × competence × effort

Your monthly earning is transparent and formula-driven — everyone can see how it's calculated in Ubuntu Town OS.

The formula

Your share = (your role weight × your band multiplier) ÷ town team total × Team Pool (45% of town revenue)  +  activation stipend for the points, nodes & workpacks you personally delivered.

Competence multiplier (from your assessment band)

BandMultiplierWhat it reflects
A×1.30Top competence — leads a Launch town
A-×1.20Strong — leads or deputises in a Launch town
B / B+×1.10–1.15Solid — deputy or lead in a Pilot town
C / C+×1.00–1.05Capable — ambassador, path to deputy
D×0.90Developing — ambassador with mentoring

Effort is the third lever: the activation stipend rewards work actually done (points activated, nodes stood up, workpacks approved), so a hungry Band-C ambassador can out-earn a passive higher band. Bands are not a ceiling — re-assessment every quarter moves you up as your proof grows.

07 — The revenue-share agreement

Your share, in writing

A participation agreement — not employment — so it stays self-funding and fair. Headline terms:

  • Participation, not employment. You earn a share of town revenue you help generate, plus activity stipends — not a fixed salary.
  • The split. 45% Town Team Pool · 30% Town Reinvestment · 25% Ubuntu Town OS. Fixed and transparent.
  • Your slice. Role weight × band multiplier ÷ town team total, paid monthly with a full statement in Ubuntu Town OS.
  • Live-and-earn. Your share tracks the Opportunity Points & nodes that stay active — keep them alive, keep earning.
  • Rise by proof. Quarterly re-assessment; move up a band and a role as your delivery grows. No equity, no dilution.
  • The code. No displacement (vacant/willing shops only), political neutrality, honest proof. Break it, forfeit the share.
  • Source of truth. Every signal, workpack, proof and payout is recorded in Ubuntu Town OS (Supabase) — one transparent ledger.
